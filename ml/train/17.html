A Weekend In The Country





































































Running hither and yon.  And this is what I'm wearing from my Top 20 wardrobe:

1.) Black cashmere T-shirt.

2.) White T-shirt beneath the sweater.

3.) Black wool crepe pants.

4.) Black patent ballerinas.

5.) Gold creoles.

6.) My watch.

When wearing my long, black redingote -- the only coat I'm wearing for this exercise -- I added a large wool gauze scarf in a gray blue with large black polka dots.

Et voila. A demain.

(I saw a crow sitting on a building on rue de Faubourg-St. Honore yesterday. That was a first. Pigeons, yes, plenty of them. A crow, never, except in our garden.)

What's Age Got To Do With It?

So what if the last time we saw 40 was on a speedometer.

C'est la vie .

It's not about the numbers. No one knows this better than Frenchwomen. Living a beautiful life is about style, elegance, discipline, simplicity and generosity.

A Femme d'Un Certain Age is dedicated to women 40ish to whateverish and every detail herein is chosen for you -- for us -- to wear, to amuse, to use. . . And always, always with a French twist.

Stay with me and I guarantee you will re-think age, beauty, well-being and style.

Together we are about to embark on a life-enhancing cultural exchange with the masters of looking fabulous at every age: Les Femmes Françaises.
